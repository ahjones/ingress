Ingress Invites

Share your Google Ingress invitations

Google Ingress is here. Don't get left out, sign up now to share invitation codes.

How it works

IngresInvites.com is an invitation exchange site. Type your email address into the box on the left and click 'Sign up' to join the invitation waiting list.

When you get to the front of the queue we'll send you an invitation code. You'll be able to use the invitation code to sign up to the game.

Google will give game players invitaitons to share with their friends. When they give you an invitation code you come back here and share them with others.
